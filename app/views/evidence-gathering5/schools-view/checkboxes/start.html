Provide supporting information for Disability Living Allowance (DLA) for children

We have received an application to claim Disability Living Allowance for Children from the parent or guardian of the child named in the email that we sent you.

As part of their application, they provided details about Evie's school in case we needed further information.

The parent or guardian of the child gave their consent to us contacting you about the child.

Before you start

You will need the reference number from the email we sent you.

You will also need to tell us information about the child, including:

  • the level of support and help they need while at school
  • any education plans they may have in place
  • their ability to move around school
  • their ability to move around school
  • their behaviour at school
  • details of any therapy or medication they need while at school
