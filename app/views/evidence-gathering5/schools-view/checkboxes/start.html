Provide supporting information for Disability Living Allowance (DLA) for children

Use this service to respond to our request for further information.

We have already received some information from the child’s parent or guardian as part of their application.

However, we need some more information about the child’s day to day needs to help us make a decision.

The information you provide will help us to understand the level of support that the child needs.

This information may also be seen by their parent or guardian upon request.

Before you start

You will need the reference number from the email we sent you.

We will ask about:

  • any individual plans in place
  • any additional learning support that they get, or would benefit from
  • their behaviour at school
  • any support they need to move around at school
  • how they manage their own personal safety
  • how they manage their own personal care
  • any therapy or medication while at school
  • any sleep problems

Using the service

If you are inactive for 15 minutes while using the service, we will delete your answers for your security, and you will need to start again.
