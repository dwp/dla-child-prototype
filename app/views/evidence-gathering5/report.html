Report details

History of conditions

 

Primary condition: Autism

He was diagnosed with Autism and ADHD in 2019 and has had the symptoms for about 3-4 years following a tick bite. He saw the GP at the time and was given antibiotics, but after this it got worse and worse, he revisited the GP with his symptoms several times.

He was sent for a scan on his head which came back as fine. His previous GP diagnosed him with fibromyalgia. His symptoms include legs feeling like pins and needles, stinging, legs feel heavy and arms also feel heavy and stinging. His head can have sensations of pins and needles. He has pain in the arms and legs everyday. He states that he gets acid reflux and certain foods make this worse.

He experiences tiredness easily but also doesn't sleep well due to pain. He has good and bad days with this, he feels her bad days are about 4 days out of 7 where his pain is worse. He uses pain relief for this. No other treatment such as physiotherapy or acupuncture.

Secondary condition: Anxiety

He has some anxiety that just makes him feel hot and on edge at certain times of the day and just feels he misses how he used to be.

No input from the community mental health team, no psychiatrist or mental health nurse. No history of suicidal thoughts.
