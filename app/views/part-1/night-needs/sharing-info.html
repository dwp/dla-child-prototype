Sharing information about the child's medical condition with DWP

We might need to ask your child's doctor or other healthcare professional for relevant information about your child's medical condition.

You do not have to give your consent. If you do not, we will make a decision based on information we already have, or which you tell us about.

How we use this information?

We use this information to:

  • process your claim more quickly
  • make a decision on your claim, or any mandatory reconsideration or appeal you make

If you change your mind

You can tell us that you want to give or withdraw your consent by calling the Disability Living Allowance for Children helpline on 0800 121 4600.
